~Painless Props - ~Streets Package~
~by Little Arms Studios, LLC~

This Painless Props Streets Package contains low-poly models, textures, materials, and prefabs of various streets. It also contains a showcase scene as an example of how the props might be used to decorate a scene.

How to Use:
To place a Painless Prop in your scene, open the Prefabs folder in your Project Pane under Painless Props/Streets. Click your desired object prefab and drag it into your scene.
